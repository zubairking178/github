About university

Pre-university history (1903-2017): The foundation for Sharnbasva University was laid by the Sharanabasveshwar Vidya Vardhak Sangha (SVVS), an educational organization established in 1903. The SVVS had a long history of service, including the establishment of the first school for girls in the region in 1934.
University establishment (2012-2017): The process for establishing the university began with the Sharnbasva University Act 2012. The university was officially established in 2017, with the Karnataka government enacting the act that year.
Location: It is the first private university in the Kalyana-Karnataka region and is located in Kalaburagi. The university is built on a 60-acre campus near Sharana Sirasagi village.
Motto: The university's motto is based on the teachings of the saint Sharanabasveshwar, promoting values of honesty, knowledge, and service.

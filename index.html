Sharnbasva University is a private, multi-disciplinary university located in Kalaburagi, Karnataka, established in 2017. It offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in fields like Engineering, Management, Science, Commerce, and Humanities. The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and is known for its focus on providing quality education at an affordable cost.
